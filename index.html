About me

I am a 29 years old developer and aesthetics lover. I was born in Warsaw, Poland and graduated from Cardinal Stefan Wyszynski University with a MA in Journalism & Media Studies. I had a chance to live in Spain before I moved to London where I have been living for five years. Before I decided to start a career in coding I worked in education and public relations, but I really desired to possess some hard skills, IT ones in particular.

In October 2017 I joined Le Wagon coding bootcamp in London, a 9-week intensive full stack program. I enjoyed learning to code in Ruby, but when we got to Front End, I completely fell in love and decided that this is what I really want to do after the bootcamp. During my spare time, I practise Tai Chi and Pilates. For me, coding has been similar to playing the piano and now I love doing both!
